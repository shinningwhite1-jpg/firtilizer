Two Generations. One Passion.

The Patriarch: Shaileshbhai Chovatiya

Since 1995, Shaileshbhai has been a revered name in the diamond trade, building a legacy on an encyclopedic knowledge of gems and an unwavering commitment to integrity. His expertise is the bedrock upon which HET IMPEX is built.

The Visionary: Het Chovatiya

Joining his father, Het brought a global perspective and a passion for design. With a vision to transform the family legacy into an international design house, he introduced the brand's signature architectural aesthetic and the innovative 'Signature Cut'.

The Art of the Signature Cut

Developed in-house, our Signature Cut features 82 facets, 24 more than a traditional brilliant cut. This unique geometry is precisely calculated to maximize light return, creating a fire and sparkle that is unmistakably HET IMPEX.

HET IMPEX

A legacy of brilliance, reimagined for the modern world. Founded by Shaileshbhai and Het Chovatiya, we sculpt light into wearable art.
